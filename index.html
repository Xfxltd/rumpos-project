About Us

The Rumpos project is a local community project that enables our

community to control cash amongst various departments and

students. In order to satisfactorily encourage a cashless policy,

we have devised this medium of seamless transaction within our

community to reduce the hassles involved when our students

access funds.

Services

The Rumpos project verifies payment made by family and friends to

the designated students. The student distributes the cash outflow

amongst departments, he/she transacts with. The departments

acknowledge receipt of cash transferred, then renders its services

to the students.

Products

The Rumpos Project helps to promote our community by showcasing some of its

developed. Skill acquisition of different types are exhibited to help our

community develop further.
